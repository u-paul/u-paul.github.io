I am a PhD Candidate in Computer Science at the University of California Santa Barbara advised by Elizabeth Belding and Arpit Gupta. My research interests include communication networks, internet measurements, networking in challenged environment and natural language processing for information extraction. The aim of my research is to understand the presence and extent of digital inequity between communities and subsequently help in mitigating the divide. I have experience of working with very large datasets, machine learning application, big data analytics and cloud computing systems (IBM Cloud, Google Cloud, AWS and Microsoft Azure). Prior to starting my PhD journey, M.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Cape Town.
